Our products

PV for Industry & Commerce

Implementing customized solutions for commercial and industrial PV installations can help improve energy system efficiency, optimize electricity costs, and reduce CO2 emissions while meeting corporate ESG sustainability goals to make sustainable contributions to our world.

PV for household

The use of a PV system with storage reduces the direct feed-in of PV electricity to the grid, which is advantageous for households due to high electricity costs. The amount of PV electricity generated is maximized for self-consumption without burdening the power grid.

Ground-mounted PV power station

The EEG 2023 represents a significant step forward for large-scale PV stations. It changes the tender model from special to general tenders and simplifies the construction of agrivoltaic Stations. This creates opportunities for the expansion of PV stations on large open spaces.

Energy storage

PV systems with integrated battery storage offer comprehensive solutions to increase local consumption of PV electricity, improve the reliability and quality of power supply, and reduce electricity bills in the long term in the process.

Charging station

The combination of PV carports and charging stations is attractive: PV electricity provides an green energy for electric vehicles, while enabling companies to demonstrate their commitment to the fight against climate change.

Heat pump

Through our Energy Management System (EMS), the green electricity generated by the PV system is efficiently used by the heat pump. The generated heat or cold is optimally distributed, which can lead to a reduction in your electricity costs.
