About us

RheinTech Energy GmbH is a renewable energy system integrator and solution provider based in Monheim am Rhein, Nordrhein-Westfalen, Germany. We provide regenerative energy products and smart energy supply solutions such as photovoltaics, energy storage, charging piles, heat pumps for domestic and commercial & industrial applications, as well as installation, operation, and maintenance services for regenerative energy systems in commercial & industrial buildings. Our team members come from renowned German University such as the Technical University of Berlin, and have many years of experience in the design, construction, and development of low carbon real estate.
