About us

RheinTech Energy (RTE) is an innovative company and one of the leading providers of renewable energy solutions for the German photovoltaic market. The team members have many years of experience in the fields of renewable energy technology and policy research as well as real estate construction and development. RTE offers a full range of services from PV plant project planning, design consulting, financing, installation to O&M, as well as energy storage, charging pile and thermal energy. as well as the integration of products such as energy storage, charging piles and heat pumps.

Our mission

We advocate altruism and are committed to promoting energy transformation, serve society with green power, and enable commercial and industrial enterprises to achieve ESG sustainability goals.
