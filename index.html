Rooftop PV for commercial & industrial buildings

Implementing customized solutions for commercial and industrial rooftop PV can help improve energy system efficiency, reduce electricity costs, reduce CO2 emissions, while achieving corporate ESG sustainability goals.

Learn more

PV for household

Due to the high cost of household electricity, the use of a PV system with storage system solution reduces the direct feed-in of PV electricity to the grid (the German PV feed-in tariff is around 9 cents) and maximizes the amount of PV electricity generated for self-consumption without burdening the grid.

Learn more

Ground-mounted photovoltaic power plants

The 2023 Renewable Energy Act (EEG) is a major benefit to the development of Agro-Photovoltaik (APV) in Germany, changing the bidding model for APV plants from a special tender to a general tender and simplifying the complexities of APV plant construction.

Charging station

Heat pump
