Creating A Green New World

Who are we？

RheinTech Energy GmbH is a renewable energy system integrator and solution provider based in Monheim am Rhein, Nordrhein-Westfalen, Germany. We provide regenerative energy products and smart energy supply solutions such as photovoltaics, energy storage, charging piles, heat pumps for domestic and commercial & industrial applications, as well as installation, operation, and maintenance services for regenerative energy systems in commercial & industrial buildings.
Our team members come from renowned German University such as the Technical University of Berlin, and have many years of experience in the design, construction, and development of low carbon real estate.
Why RheinTech Energy?
Real estate developers and general contractors alike face a challenge: the complexity of integrating new energy sources into existing buildings or new construction. the RheinTech Energy team has qualified energy management consultants who can provide technical and policy consulting services during the planning and design phase of a project, creating room for your own core business. We can provide a full range of quality services from project planning and design consulting, renewable energy product procurement, installation, operation and maintenance, and financing solutions, leaving you with no worries.
